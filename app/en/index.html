Main features

UP Hiring is Future of Video Interviews

The first job search platform that allows you to pass an online interview

Choose a version convenient for you

Web version for PC and smartphones or install a mobile application for Android or iOS

Manage your profile through your personal account

Create a resume, add documents proving your experience and qualifications, set up a profile privacy

Video interview - everything you need at your fingertips

See interviews in real time, take notes, view summaries and attachments. Assess the convenience of a ready-made solution for fast and efficient communication

A platform that opens up new opportunities

Add to favorites, subscribe to notifications, receive mailings about new vacancies, candidates, companies and news. Find friends and colleagues, share career achievements, join to groups

UP HIRING the best assistant to the employer

Use the interview calendar, keep up to date with all the changes thanks to the convenient notification system. Create and save unique search filters for each vacancy

Mutual evaluation of job seekers and employers

This option makes it possible to screen out unscrupulous employers and create jobseekers' ratings. This allows you to quickly find the best candidates and held interviews in the audited companies.

Intelligent Calendar-Interviews

Appointment of a date / time with the possibility to change, control of confirmation, reminders of changes, quick review of the resume, auto notification of candidates about the closure of the vacancy.

Structuring resume according to the search query

Information, relevant vacancies, displayed at the top of the resume, everything else is displayed below. Save time when selecting candidates
